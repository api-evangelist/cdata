Databricks' 2026 summit centers on the challenge of building AI agents that work with enterprise data, a problem CData will address at Booth #126. CData offers three solutions: Connect AI for managed MCP connectivity to live enterprise systems, Sync for automated data movement into Databricks, and Drivers for direct JDBC, ODBC, and Python connectivity. The piece highlights how fragmented enterprise data landscapes — spanning legacy databases, ERP platforms, and SaaS apps — create barriers that prevent agents from reliably accessing the data they need.
